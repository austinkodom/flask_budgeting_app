How to use the Budgeting App

Welcome to my Budgeting App! Follow these steps to manage your finances effectively:

1. Sign Up & Log In

Create an account by signing up. If you already have an account, simply log in.

2. Add Incomes

For every income source in the household add what you expect to bring in.
If you haven't actually received that income yet, that is okay! Leave the
actual tab at zero for now. As you receive the income, go ahead and fill
that section in with what was actually received by typing that amount in
the actual section and pressing the 'edit' buttion.
When you are finished adding the expected amounts for income, the dashboard
should show your total budgetable income. This number is the total amount
that you are expected to bring in and can budget for.

3. Add Expenses

Navigate to the expenses page and do the same as you did for the income
section. Add each expected expense you will have for the month and you
can add the actual amounts later as you make those payments. Once you
are finished, your "left remaining" amount should be zero. You want to
give every dollar amount a place to go. If you go over, then adjust your
budget to work for the month. If you have some left over, just add it
to savings or some other place you would like that money to go.

4. View your Progress

The dashboard on each page will show you the total budgetable amount,
the amount leftover (or amount you went over) to budget for, the actual
amount you have spent for the month (real totals), and the over/under
you have spent versus what has came in.

5. Update or Delete Entries

The purpose of this app is to update the entries as you go so you can
have an idea of what you are actually spending against your budget.
You can also delete entries as needed.

6. Log Out

Click the logout button to securely log out from the app.
